About

Dirty Blood: A blood cell classifier
The diagnosis of blood-based diseases often involve identifying and characterizing patient blood samples. Automated methods to detect and classify blood subtypes have important medical applications. Our CNN-based model does the same and classifies the blood cells into four categories i.e [Eosinophil, Lymphocyte, Monocyte, Neutrophil] with an accuracy of 86%.
